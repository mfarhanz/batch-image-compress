Privacy Policy

WebPly is designed to prioritize user privacy. This policy explains how your data is handled when using the WebPly image compression tool.

Client-Side Processing

All image compression and conversion performed by WebPly occurs directly within your web browser. Images you upload are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

Data Collection

WebPly does not collect, store, or transmit the images you process using the tool. No uploaded files leave your device.

Standard website analytics or logs from the hosting platform may collect limited technical information such as browser type or general usage statistics. This information does not include the images you upload.

Cookies

WebPly may store small preference values locally in your browser, such as theme settings (light or dark mode). These preferences remain on your device and are not shared with external services.

Third-Party Services

WebPly may link to external websites for informational purposes. These sites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected on this page with a revised date if applicable.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy, you can contact the developer at mfarhanz.dev/contact.
